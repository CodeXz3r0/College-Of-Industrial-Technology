Our Programs

We offer specialized courses that nurture technical skills and creativity across multiple industrial fields.

Major in Automotive Technology

Focuses on automotive servicing, engine systems, and mechanical diagnostics.

Major in Computer Technology

Trains students in programming, hardware maintenance, and IT solutions.

Major in Electrical Technology

Covers power systems, wiring design, and electrical safety practices.

Major in Electronics Technology

Specializes in circuitry, instrumentation, and digital electronics.

Major in Food Technology

Teaches food processing, preservation, and quality assurance techniques.

Major in Garments Technology

Develops expertise in clothing design, production, and tailoring methods.

Major in Industrial Design Technology

Combines creativity and engineering in product and design development.

Major in Mechanical Technology

Focuses on manufacturing processes, machining, and mechanical systems.

About the College

The College of Industrial Technology is committed to producing globally competitive graduates equipped with both knowledge and practical skills. Our programs are built on innovation, craftsmanship, and a dedication to technological excellence.
